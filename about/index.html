About

The future of discovery is AI. We help you show up in it.

For two decades, companies competed for rankings in search engines. That model is rapidly changing. Buyers now ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Google AI Overviews to recommend products and vendors — and receive a short list of specific companies in return. If your brand isn't in those answers, you're invisible to a growing share of your market.

Traditional search

Companies compete for rankings and clicks

You optimized pages, ranked for keywords, and users browsed links. Dozens of companies could appear on page one.

→

AI-driven discovery

AI synthesizes information and recommends specific companies

Users ask a question and receive a direct answer — with a small set of companies named. Those recommendations increasingly determine which vendors get evaluated, shortlisted, and hired.

Our founders

Built by people who have lived every major shift in how buyers find information

Frank Days

Co-founder, Fix My AI Rank · Co-founder, TangySlice Collective

Frank Days has spent more than 20 years helping fast-growing technology companies turn marketing into a growth engine. Most recently, he served as CMO at Aerospike, where he drove 50% new customer acquisition growth, launched an AI thought leadership program, and led a full brand transformation. Before that, as SVP of Marketing at EDB, he helped scale the company from $25M to $100M in ARR over five years, culminating in a successful Bain Capital exit. Earlier roles at Tesora and TwinStrata sharpened his instinct for repositioning and narrative — both companies were successfully acquired.

Today, Frank applies that same strategic lens to the challenge of AI search visibility. As buyers increasingly turn to large language models to discover and evaluate solutions, the rules of being found have fundamentally changed. At Fix My AI Rank, he works with companies to make sure their expertise — not just their keywords — gets surfaced when it matters most. He holds an MBA from MIT Sloan and a BA from Bowdoin College, and has co-hosted the Marketing Agility Podcast for over a decade.

Tim Dailey

Co-founder, Fix My AI Rank · Co-founder, TangySlice Collective

Tim Dailey has spent more than 20 years at the intersection of digital marketing, web strategy, and search — long enough to have lived through every major shift in how buyers find information online. He has led digital marketing at organizations ranging from high-growth B2B technology companies like Aerospike and EDB to Citizens Bank, where he served in a VP-level web strategy role. His 12+ years at ForHealth Consulting (UMass Medical School) gave him a deep grounding in content credibility and audience trust — qualities that matter more than ever in the age of AI search.

Tim's focus in recent years has been on the emerging discipline of Generative Engine Optimization: understanding why some brands get cited by AI models and others get ignored entirely. At Fix My AI Rank, he helps companies build the kind of structured, authoritative, and conversationally relevant content that earns a seat at the table when a language model answers a buyer's question. His background in CRO and UX means the work doesn't stop at visibility — it connects all the way through to conversion.
