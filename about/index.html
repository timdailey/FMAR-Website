About

The future of discovery is AI.

We help you show up in it.

For two decades, companies competed for rankings in search engines. That model is changing fast. Buyers now ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Google AI Overviews to recommend products and vendors. They get back a short list of specific companies. If your brand isn't on that list, you're invisible to a growing share of your market.

Traditional search

Companies compete for rankings and clicks

You optimized pages, ranked for keywords, and users browsed links. Dozens of companies could appear on page one.

→

AI-driven discovery

AI synthesizes information and recommends specific companies

Users ask a question and get a direct answer with a small set of companies named. Those recommendations shape which vendors get evaluated, shortlisted, and hired.

Our founders

Built by people who have lived every major shift in how buyers find information

Frank Days

Co-founder, Fix My AI Rank · Co-founder, TangySlice Collective

Frank Days has spent more than 20 years helping fast-growing technology companies turn marketing into a growth engine. Most recently, he served as CMO at Aerospike, where he drove 50% new customer acquisition growth, launched an AI thought leadership program, and led a full brand transformation. Before that, as SVP of Marketing at EDB, he helped scale the company from $25M to $100M in ARR over five years into a successful Bain Capital exit. Earlier roles at Tesora and TwinStrata, both successfully acquired, sharpened his eye for repositioning and narrative.

Today, Frank brings that same strategic focus to AI search visibility. As buyers turn to large language models to discover and evaluate solutions, the rules of being found have changed. At Fix My AI Rank, he works with companies to make sure their expertise gets in front of AI systems when it matters most. He holds an MBA from MIT Sloan and a BA from Bowdoin College, and has co-hosted the Marketing Agility Podcast for over a decade.

Tim Dailey

Co-founder, Fix My AI Rank · Co-founder, TangySlice Collective

Tim Dailey has spent more than 20 years at the intersection of digital marketing, web strategy, and search. He has led digital marketing at organizations ranging from high-growth B2B technology companies like Aerospike and EDB to Citizens Bank, where he served in a VP-level web strategy role. At Fix My AI Rank, he helps companies build structured, authoritative, conversationally relevant content that gets named when a language model answers a buyer's question. His background in CRO and UX means the work doesn't stop at visibility. It carries through to conversion.
