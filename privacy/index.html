1. Introduction

FixMyAIRank.com ("we," "us," or "our") is operated by Tangyslice Collective LLC (pending formation), a business based in Massachusetts, USA. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use our free online AI visibility research service (the "Service").

Important notice regarding data sharing: When you submit our form, you will have the option to consent to having your contact and company information shared with trusted third-party B2B marketing partners. This sharing is opt-in — you will not be enrolled unless you check the consent box on our submission form. See Section 6.1 for full details and how to withdraw consent at any time.

By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you submit a research request through our Service, we collect:

  • Contact information: Your email address
  • Company information: Your company name and company website URL
  • Competitor information: Up to three competitor website URLs you provide
  • Form responses: Any additional information you include in your submission

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on the Service
  • Referring URLs

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate and improve our website. Cookies are small data files stored on your device.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic website functionality (session management, security). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site (pages visited, time on site, referral sources). This data is aggregated and anonymized. You may opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On.
  • Functional cookies: Used by our WordPress platform to remember preferences and improve your experience.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of the Service. For EU/UK users, we will seek your consent before placing non-essential cookies.

2.4 Information From Third Parties

To generate your AI visibility report, we retrieve publicly available information about your company and competitors from third-party sources, including web search results, public business directories, and review platforms. This information is used solely to produce your report.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To deliver the Service: Processing your submission and generating your AI visibility research report
  • To send your report: Emailing the completed report to the address you provide
  • For research and analytics: Analyzing aggregated, de-identified data to improve our research methodology, understand industry trends, and develop our Service
  • For marketing communications: With your consent (or as permitted by applicable law), sending you information about our services, updates, or related offerings
  • To share with marketing partners: Where you have provided consent via our submission form, sharing your registration information with trusted third-party B2B marketing partners. See Section 6.1 for details.
  • To comply with legal obligations: Responding to lawful requests from authorities and protecting our legal rights

4. How We Process Your Report Data

To generate your AI visibility report, we use third-party AI language model services and web data tools. Your submitted company name and website URL, along with competitor URLs you provide, may be processed by these third-party AI services as part of the report generation process. We do not share your personal contact information (such as your email address) with these AI processing services. All third-party AI tools we use are governed by their own data processing terms and privacy policies.

5. Legal Bases for Processing (EU/UK Users)

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom, we process your personal data under the following legal bases under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK GDPR:

  • Performance of a contract: Processing necessary to deliver the Service you requested (Article 6(1)(b))
  • Legitimate interests: Research, analytics, and improving our Service, where our interests do not override your rights (Article 6(1)(f))
  • Consent: Marketing communications and cookie placement where consent is required (Article 6(1)(a)). You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal obligation: Where we are required to process data to comply with applicable law (Article 6(1)(c))

6.1 Third-Party B2B Marketing Partners

With your explicit consent — provided by checking the opt-in box on our submission form — we may share your registration information with trusted third-party B2B marketing partners, such as software vendors, agencies, and marketing technology providers. What we do not do: We do not sell your data to data brokers or consumer marketing lists. We do not share your data with third-party marketing partners without your prior consent.

Your consent options:

  • Opt in: Check the consent box on our submission form to allow this sharing

We will process your request within 10 business days. Withdrawing consent does not affect any sharing that occurred prior to your withdrawal.

EU/UK users: We will only share your data with third-party marketing partners where you have provided prior explicit consent via the opt-in checkbox on our submission form, consistent with our obligations under GDPR Article 6(1)(a). You may withdraw consent at any time as described above.

California users: You have the right to opt out of the sharing of your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. To exercise this right, contact us at removeme@fixmyairank.com with the subject line "California — Opt Out of Sharing."

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.
