About AI visibility

  • AI visibility refers to how often and how accurately your brand appears in answers generated by AI platforms like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini. When a buyer asks one of those platforms to recommend vendors in your category, AI visibility determines whether your name makes the list.

  • Search engines and AI platforms use different signals. Google ranks pages based on backlinks and keyword relevance. AI systems generate answers based on entity clarity, structured data, third-party validation, and content structure. A brand can hold the top Google ranking for its most important keyword and still be completely absent from AI-generated answers. The two are separate problems.

  • The report tests your brand across ChatGPT (GPT-4o), Perplexity AI, and Claude. These are the three platforms where B2B buyers are most actively using AI to research and shortlist vendors.
