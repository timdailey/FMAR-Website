Why this matters

Local Buyers Are Asking AI Before They Ask Google

A growing share of "who should I call" decisions now start in ChatGPT, Perplexity, or a Google AI Overview, not a list of ten blue links.

What is local AI visibility?

How often, and how accurately, your business is named when someone asks an AI platform a location-based question like "best HVAC company near me." It's the local map pack, reborn as a conversational answer.

Why local SEO isn't enough anymore

A strong Google ranking doesn't guarantee an AI recommendation. AI platforms weigh directory consistency, Google Business Profile completeness, and independent third-party confirmation, not just your own site.

What's at stake

Most buyers now use generative AI to research purchases. A customer down the street can search for exactly what you offer and never see your name if your local signals aren't in place.

The good news: it's fixable

Local AI visibility comes down to a short, well-defined list of signals. No rebuild required, just knowing exactly which ones are missing for your business.
